Main Logistics specialises in providing diverse transportation solutions within the container industry. Our comprehensive services involve container deliveries across the entirety of the UK and originating from major ports.

Our primary objective is to ensure the timely delivery of your goods, coupled with transparent communication regarding any delays or obstacles encountered throughout the transportation process.

With a proven track record, we consistently meet the seasonal and year-round demands of a substantial customer base. Our capabilities extend to handling a wide spectrum of cargo, ranging from dry stock to refrigerated goods.

Our transport network constantly being developed bringing more sustainable traffic suitable to your requirements. Our goal is to support your business wherever your load is located across UK.

We are working 7 days a week to support your requirement and to make sure your load is fully tracked. Our team is specialised to answer all your question you may have at any time any day 24/7.

Our consistent growth its improved by adding dedicated subcontractors this means we are your local haulage at your door step therefore we will be always competitive with the price.

We have a team of specialist working in transport and logistics trade with over 5 years experience mainly concentrating on containers market but we are also helping customers with temperature controlled loads and curtain sider pallet work. Our diversity and flexibility in planning a load provides fully comprehensive service.
