Welcome to Main Logistics LTD

Main Logistics revolutionizes the haulage market by offering an extensive network of transport companies throughout the UK. With a current capacity of over 50 daily deliveries, we take pride in consistently delivering exceptional service to both our valued customers and our growing roster of designated hauliers.

This expansion ensures heightened transport flexibility. Our innovative approach focuses on cost reduction within the transportation sector. Through meticulous route selection and optimal starting points for deliveries, we strive to minimize expenses.

For quotations or inquiries, please do not hesitate to contact us. Our experienced team is well-equipped to furnish you with the most accurate pricing and delivery dates tailored to your specific needs.

Main Logistics specialises in providing diverse transportation solutions within the container industry. Our comprehensive services involve container deliveries across the entirety of the UK and originating from major ports.

Our primary objective is to ensure the timely delivery of your goods, coupled with transparent communication regarding any delays or obstacles encountered throughout the transportation process.

With a proven track record, we consistently meet the seasonal and year-round demands of a substantial customer base. Our capabilities extend to handling a wide spectrum of cargo, ranging from dry stock to refrigerated goods.
